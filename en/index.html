ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Mardony Porto and I am a designer graduated from Federal University of Campina Grande (UFCG).

During my studies, I fell in love with UX/UI Design and the development of user-centered solutions. I also explored front-end fundamentals, which helps me design more feasible interfaces aligned with both user experience and implementation.

My professional journey has included different roles — such as data entry clerk, IT technician, call center representative, and independent automotive parts sales representative, my family's business.

These experiences shaped the way I see people, processes, and problems, strengthening skills such as organization, clear communication, working with deadlines, and practical problem-solving.

Today, I aim to work as a Product Designer, combining technical expertise and human sensitivity to create efficient, accessible digital products with real impact on people's experiences.
